Strengthening Regulatory Cybersecurity Compliance for Financial Institutions

The Central Bank of the UAE (CBUAE) mandates licensed financial institutions to comply with the Information Assurance Regulation (IAR) and national cybersecurity requirements derived from the UAE Information Assurance Standards (formerly NESA framework). Our CBUAE NESA / IAR consultancy services are designed to help banks, finance companies, exchange houses, insurance providers, and fintech entities achieve structured, regulator-ready compliance with confidence.
